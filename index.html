Research Experience

My research centers on interpretable mechanisms in deep learning—uncovering how neural networks encode, transform, and utilize representations to make decisions. I am particularly interested in leveraging sparse autoencoders and mechanistic interpretability tools to disentangle latent features and understand the inner workings of both classical architectures and large language models.
